About Us

OUR DESIGN PRINCIPLES

At Design Plus Group, we focus on bringing creativity, technical excellence, and environmental sustainability to every landscape project.

We advocate for forward-thinking spatial designs to help communities and environments thrive in perfect harmony.

LANDSCAPE DESIGN

We blend creative spatial flows with sustainable landscape designs tailored specifically to contextual local flora, site levels, and topography.

SUSTAINABILITY

Delivering outstanding modern landscape solutions that blend native plants, water efficiency, and eco-friendly features to protect and enhance green environments.

ARCHITECTURAL HARMONY

We view landscape and architecture as a single, unified expression. We structure transitions, elevations, and materials to ensure that built spaces breathe naturally side-by-side with nature.

OUR BIM VISION

Our BIM modeling workflow enables multi-disciplinary design and coordination with engineers, contractors and architects worldwide.
