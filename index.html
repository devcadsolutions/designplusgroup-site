About Us.

OUR DESIGN PRINCIPLES

At RAMA ARCHITECTURE STUDIO we focus on client's essential and functional requirements without sacrificing on aesthetics, quality, safety and project impact on its environment.

We also advocate on "forward design principles" to help our clients maximize the project's potential for future growth and expansions.

FUNCTIONALITY

We design with spatial function as first priority thus enabling spaces to be utilized well. We also introduce solutions to make spaces future ready, expandable for mixed use and adaptable to multiple functions beyond its purpose.

SUSTAINABILITY

We continue to search and apply sustainable materials and techniques for all our projects to support local products and building materials while reducing the project's environmental impact.

RESILIENCE

We maximize site orientation to match appropriate spaces and use necessary materials and solutions to take advantage of the site's characteristics.

ORGANIC

We utilize the site's character and topography as a design feature thus blending with nature and environment while reducing materials & resources requirements for the projects.
